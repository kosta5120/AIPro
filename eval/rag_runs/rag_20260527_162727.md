## Answer

BM25 is a robust extension of TF-IDF used heavily in modern search engines and RAG retrieval pipelines because it saturates term frequency to prevent term spamming [pdf_AI_Developers_Course_Textbook-v2_sec70_chunk_83]. Hybrid search combines dense vector search with sparse BM25 search, utilizing a Cross-Encoder for precise ranking [pdf_AI_Developers_Course_Textbook-v2_sec160_chunk_190].
